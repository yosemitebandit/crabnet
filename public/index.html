about

Clinicians and Pharmaceutical companies need new methodologies for finding eligible candidates for clinical trials.

48% of clinical trials fail to reach recruitment targets.

Treatment development costs $1B USD per year.

Only 18% of participants in clinical trials found their options online.

methods

We employ a diverse set of strategies to inform potential candidates about clinical trials for which they may be eligible:

  • We manage targeted Facebook and Google ad campaigns to rapidly seek out candidates.
  • Our researcher network informs us of patients seeking trials and is kept constantly up-to-date via our open source data-mining algorithms.
  • A custom recruitment panel is available for embedding in advocacy websites and patient forums to keep patients informed.
